campus life

school library

Was Established in 1990

One day, it was our library period and and we were in our library reading books. Me and my friends had decided in the class only that we all will sit in the same row beside each and will have a talk while hiding our faces with the books. I was just holding the book in front of my face and our teacher came and asked me that, "Srajan are you reading the book ?" Since I was talking with my friends I didn't pay attention that I was holding the book upside-down and replied ,"Yes Mam" and then I realised that what I had done. After that we all were scolded by the teacher.

What's new

  • Automatic sensor sanitizing machines applied in school due to COVID-19 threat

    As schools have now opened in this threat of COVID-19. Our Central Government has strictly ordered all the schools to follow the guidelines. All the students of our school are following them properly. To keep their hands clean and free from germs our school has implemented Automatic sensor sanitizing machines in the premises. Now our students can just sanitize their hands without touching anything and I appreciate this step of our school.

  • Dr. VSEC, Kidwai Nagar shifts to online classes on Microsoft Teams to the Year 2020 due to the threat of COVID-19

    During this Covid-19 Pandemic as there was lockdown schools were shut down and our studies were at a brake so our school found out an new way to study - online studies. Our school decided to take online classes at Microsoft Teams.Our teachers conducted training sessions on MS Teams and after one to two weeks we were ready for online classes on MS Teams. At present our classes are going well on the software and we all are enjoying them.
